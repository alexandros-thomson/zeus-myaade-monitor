## Executive Summary

This document escalates the Kyprianos v. AADE Protocol Oversight case to EPPO following the failure of the Hellenic Ministry of Digital Governance to respond within the statutory deadline of **21 February 2026** (extended trigger: **06/03/2026**). The case involves systematic cadastral fraud, IBAN audit obstruction, and multi-agency non-compliance affecting EU financial interests.

Four coordinated fronts were executed on **28 February 2026** (Night Ops, 8:12 PM – 1:21 AM EST), generating six new tracker rows (241–246) across Ktimatologio, Ministry of Digital Governance, Cybercrime Division, and EAD (AEAD). As of 06/03/2026, the Ministry of Digital Governance has not responded to Protocol 4633/4505/4314, triggering escalation to EPPO, Synigoros tou Politi, and OLAF/FBI notification per the pre-established trigger calendar.

All protocol numbers listed below are drawn from the **28/02/2026 Night Ops debrief** (`evidence/2026-02-28-night-ops-debrief.md`, rows 241–246).
